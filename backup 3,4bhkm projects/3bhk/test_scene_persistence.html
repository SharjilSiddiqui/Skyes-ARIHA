  • Navigate to different rooms on the 17th floor

  • The system should maintain the same room type when switching floors
Example: If you're in "bedroom01" on the 17th floor and switch to the 28th floor, you should automatically go to "bedroom01" on the 28th floor.

How It Works

  1. The system tracks your current floor and room type
  2. When you switch floors, it finds the corresponding room on the new floor
  3. If the exact room doesn't exist, it defaults to the entrance
  4. Your scene state is saved to localStorage for persistence across page reloads
  5. The floor navigation buttons highlight the current floor
